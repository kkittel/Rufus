Introduction

    Everyone has heard of Artificial Intelligence these days. It's one of those things that you see in the movies, and it makes so much sense, you just know that one day it will be invented by someone. Well I got tired of waiting on someone else to invent it. I wanted something that I could tinker with and bring some of my ideas about AI to life. For one thing, I felt that the traditional approaches to achieve AI were either too simple or too complicated. Take chatbots for instance. They are too simple. Tell a chatbot your name. Then immediately ask it your name. Most of the simple chatbots will fail. On the other hand, if an AI program is trying to diagram a sentence the way your english teacher taught you, I feel that this is over complicated. The human mind doesn't learn language that way, why should a computer?
